Verified Purchase

I was skeptical about about buying these earbuds because of the brand and the price, but what attracted my attention was the product description. So, I decided to give it a try, because I was looking for something budget friendly and that also delivers good quality on audio, connectivity, and that actually fit my ears.

I was surprised that they actually have an excellent audio quality, they are responsive to the touch, the range of connectivity (distance between the phone and the earbuds) is great, and they are easy to use, especially if you are familiar with the apple brand. They are really comfortable, and I can tell that, because I have this problem with my ear canal to be narrow and other brands don't stay in place, no matter the ear tip size, but I tried the smallest ear tips that were provided, and they really stay in place for hours , so I was worry free doing my activities.

I live in a dessert area, so I haven't tried the waterproof feature, but I can tell for sure that they are sweat proof. I didn't notice any interference or any issues while working in my roof or yard. The battery life is awesome too, they are truly light weight, and the size is very convenient.

So, if you need something reliable, budget friendly, pretty, that delivers a great audio quality, and have a good range of connectivity, seek no more, this is your product.
